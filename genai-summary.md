# Gen AI Summary
## How it works
Through typing in your response through the text-based user interface, the chatbot will give you a rundown of the financial details over the past one or two years. The chatbot will send a predefined error handing message if a response is unknown. The current predefined responses also takes into consideration capitalization.

## Predefined Queries
It can respond to questions highlight the total revenue and net income and how it changed over the last year. For total assets, it can respond to the changes over the last two years.

## Limitations of the current design
The current responses don't differentiate between the three companies (Microsoft, Tesla, Apple) and doesn't take into consideration mishaps in spelling the sentence, any derivative of the sentence that means the same thing, without puncuation, and any other additional  information such as liabilities, etc.
